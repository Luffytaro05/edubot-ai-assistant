Our History

Tanauan City College was established with the vision of providing quality and accessible higher education to the residents of Tanauan City and neighboring communities.

Over the years, TCC has grown from a small institution to a comprehensive college offering diverse academic programs that meet the needs of our students and the demands of the workforce.

Today, TCC stands as a testament to the city's commitment to educational excellence and community development, continuously evolving to provide the best learning experience for our students.

Our Core Values

These principles guide our actions, decisions, and relationships within our academic community.

Professionalism

We pursue the highest standards of performance in all our academic and administrative endeavors, constantly striving to exceed expectations and achieve outstanding results.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our actions, fostering an environment of trust and accountability within our college community.

Value for Excellence

We embrace creativity and forward-thinking approaches to education, encouraging the development of new ideas and solutions to address evolving challenges.

Open for Innovation

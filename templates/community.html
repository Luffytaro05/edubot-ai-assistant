How to Join an Organization

1
Explore Available Organizations

Browse through the different student organizations available at TCC. You can find information about:

  • TCC ILC - Iskolar ng Lungsod Council
  • TCC MISU - Management Information Systems Unit
  • Academic Clubs and Organizations
  • Cultural Organizations
  • Sports Clubs
2
Attend Organization Meetings or Events

Visit the organization's Facebook page or attend their orientation meetings to learn more about their activities, goals, and membership requirements. This will help you understand if the organization aligns with your interests.

3
Contact the Organization

Reach out to the organization through their official Facebook page or contact the Office of Student Affairs (OSA). You can:

  • Send a message via their Facebook page
  • Visit the OSA office at the Student Center, Room 305
  • Attend organization recruitment events
4
Submit Your Application

Complete the membership application form provided by the organization. Be prepared to provide:

  • Your full name and Student ID
  • Contact information (email and phone number)
  • A brief statement about why you want to join
  • Any relevant skills or interests you can contribute
5
Wait for Confirmation

After submitting your application, the organization will review it and contact you regarding your membership status. This process typically takes 1-2 weeks. Once accepted, you'll receive information about upcoming meetings and activities.
